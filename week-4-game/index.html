BlackouT Crystal Collector


A You random number will appear at bottom of crystals at start of the game.
Each of the four items below, by clicking on an item, will add an unidentified points to your total.
The game is won by matching your total to the random number. The game is a loss if your total score goes above the random number.
When game resets, the items will have a new value assigned to them.
